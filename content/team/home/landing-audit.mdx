## What the landing app is (and isn't)

**Is:** Public marketing + **account web** in one property (synap-landing): auth routes (`/(auth)/signup`, login, etc.), billing, pod lifecycle, guides ŌĆö **not** a separate ŌĆ£signup appŌĆØ repo.
**Is not:** The product. All actual knowledge management, AI, entity storage lives in the desktop app (`browser/`) and pod backend (`synap-backend/`).

The landing / account site's job is to:
1. Convert visitors into signups
2. Guide signups through pod provisioning
3. Handle transactional flows that must be web-based (email links, Stripe redirects, OAuth callbacks, invite acceptance)
4. Provide developer and user documentation

## Dark mode theme issues found and fixed

All product pages (`/product/security`, `/product/pods`, `/product/workspace`) were missing `backgroundColor: '$background'` on their `Page` styled component ŌåÆ appeared light. **Fixed.**

Text overlap in "Why this matters" (security) and "Self-hosting" (pods) and "The test" (workspace) sections was caused by `SizableText` with numeric `lineHeight` multipliers (e.g. `lineHeight={1.7}`) rendering inline ŌåÆ paragraphs overlapping. **Fixed** by replacing with `&lt;p>` tags using explicit `px` values.
