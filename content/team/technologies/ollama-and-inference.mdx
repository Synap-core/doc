## Role in Eve

- **`inference_only`** and **`full`** profiles use **Ollama** (typically on Docker **`eve-network`**) and a **Traefik-secured gateway** (e.g. **127.0.0.1:11435**) — see **hestia-cli** `README` / **`docs/EVE_SETUP_PROFILES.md`** for current ports.
- **Synap** “which model for Hub / IS” is separate from **Eve** “which provider for local OpenCode” — see **[Eve — Synap integration](/team/eve-cli/synap-integration)**.

## Upstream

- [Ollama](https://github.com/ollama/ollama) — install, models, API.

## Operations notes

- Pulling large models needs **disk and RAM**; small VPS may OOM during **`docker compose build`** for heavy services — prefer **image-based** Synap install on constrained hosts.
- GPU passthrough (NVIDIA) is **host and Docker** dependent — not Eve-specific.
