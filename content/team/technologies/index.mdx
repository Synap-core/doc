This space holds **curated references** for components we **do not own**: feed generators, inference runtimes, reverse proxies, container runtimes, and tunnels. **Behavioral truth** for Synap and Eve remains in the **backend** and **hestia-cli** repos.

**When to use this:** onboarding, debugging, or GTM explaining “what runs under the hood.” **When not to:** API contracts — use **[Platform](/team/platform/index)** and **[Architecture (public)](/docs/architecture/index)**.
