> **Draft:** Scope intentionally constrained until ownership and command set are finalized.

## Proposed scope

- fast command-entry workflows for power users
- lightweight capture and retrieval interactions
- handoff into Browser or other canonical surfaces for deep workflows

## Non-goals (current)

- replacing Browser as primary desktop shell
- duplicating full extension feature sets
- introducing alternate governance rules for writes

## Dependencies

- package/runtime dependencies in **[Synap App](/team/synap-app)**
- platform contracts in **[Platform](/team/platform)**
