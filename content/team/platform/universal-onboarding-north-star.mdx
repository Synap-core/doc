# Universal onboarding north star

This page **summarizes** the onboarding-centric roadmap (see internal **S-tier product phases** plan). It does not replace **[Feed system](/team/platform/feed-system)** or **[RSSHub](/team/technologies/rsshub)** — feeds are **one vertical** (Relay default primary goal).

## One sentence

Synap uses **one orchestration pattern** to onboard users onto a **new or current** **data pod or workspace** with a **primary goal** per app (Relay: feed, Browser: bento, Content: project), **grounded memory**, and optional **secondary objectives**, plus alternate entry points (**import everything**, **workspace templates**).
