# Decisions

Use this space to document why a decision was made, what alternatives were considered, and how we measure whether it was correct.

## What belongs here

- Architecture decisions with cross-service impact
- Product scope and prioritization decisions
- Operational policy changes (release, incident, support model)
- GTM and positioning shifts that affect execution

## Decision quality checklist

- Context is explicit
- Alternatives are listed
- Trade-offs are concrete
- Owner and review date are set
- Follow-up metrics are defined

Start from the [ADR template](/team/decisions/adr-template), then add an entry in [Decision log](/team/decisions/decision-log).
