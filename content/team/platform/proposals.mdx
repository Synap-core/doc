# Proposals & Governance

Governance ensures AI and connector mutations stay reviewable and reversible.

- `checkPermissionOrPropose()` gates write actions.
- RBAC + policy checks determine direct execution vs proposal creation.
- `DEFAULT_AUTO_APPROVE` keeps low-risk operations fast while preserving control.
