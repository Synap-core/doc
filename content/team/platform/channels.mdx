# Channels

Channels are the interaction surface between users, agents, and system events.

- 7 channel types support direct, collaborative, and system-driven workflows.
- AI execution triggers on `ai_thread` and `branch` channels.
- Every user gets a personal channel per workspace for proactive and private interactions.
