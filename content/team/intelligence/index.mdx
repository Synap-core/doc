# Intelligence Service

The IS is the AI brain of Synap. It hosts the OrchestratorAgent (co-founder, full tools) and PersonaAgent (domain expert), routes via Hub Protocol, and streams responses with Traefik sticky sessions.

**Single source of truth:** long-form guides for agents, contracts, streaming, SDK, pricing, and external agents are maintained in **this team space** (`content/team/intelligence/**/*.mdx`). The old `synap-intelligence-service/docs/*.md` tree was removed; the service repo keeps **`ARCHITECTURE.md`**, **`INTERNAL_API.md`**, **`deploy/*`**, and **monitor `inline-help/*.md`** for clone-and-run workflows.
