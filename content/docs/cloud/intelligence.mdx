---
title: Synap Intelligence
description: The agent and orchestration layer that runs on top of your Synap data pod
---

**Synap Intelligence** is the layer that lets AI **work with your entities, views, and channels** under **your governance** — not a generic chat API bolted onto a database.

Compared to a classic model provider, Synap Intelligence emphasizes:

- **Pod-native context** — agents read and act through the same APIs and permissions as your apps.
- **Proposal-first mutations** — high-impact changes can be reviewed before they land (human in the loop).
- **Hub Protocol** — one integration surface for external agents and tools (`/api/hub/*` on the data pod).

Use Synap with any model provider you choose; Intelligence is the **orchestration and policy** layer on top.
