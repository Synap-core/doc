# Notifications

Notifications unify governance actions, proactive AI nudges, and system status.

- Registry-backed types keep payloads typed and auditable.
- `NotificationService` handles persistence plus realtime fanout.
- Lifecycle actions include actioned status transitions and cleanup/expiry policies.
