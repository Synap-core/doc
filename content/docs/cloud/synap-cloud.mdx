**Product:** [synap.live](https://synap.live) · **Control plane API:** [api.synap.live](https://api.synap.live)

Synap can run entirely on **your** infrastructure. When you use Synap’s hosted offering, optional **cloud services** sit in the middle so you do not have to operate everything on day one:

- **Identity & sessions** — sign-in, workspace membership, and secure session handling aligned with your pod.
- **Security boundaries** — consistent authz checks between clients, agents, and your data pod.
- **Provisioning** — creating and linking workspaces to pods without manual wiring.

This is **not** a second product to learn: it is the **hosted control plane** described in product terms. Internal engineering details (APIs, multi-tenant model, runbooks) live in **team** documentation after sign-in.

**Getting started:** sign up at **[synap.live](https://synap.live)** — see also **[Use Synap → Installation (Hosted)](/docs/start/getting-started/installation#hosted-synap-cloud)** for how this path fits next to Docker and self-host.
