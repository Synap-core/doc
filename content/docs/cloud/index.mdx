This space combines two public-facing topics that often land together in evaluations:

- **[Synap Cloud](./synap-cloud)** — identity, security boundaries, and provisioning when you use **[synap.live](https://synap.live)** instead of self-hosting everything on day one.
- **[Synap Intelligence](./intelligence)** — how agents, proposals, and Hub Protocol sit **on top of** your pod (not a generic model API).

Team-only depth (APIs, multi-tenant model, runbooks, IS deployment) stays under **[Team](/team/overview)** after sign-in.
