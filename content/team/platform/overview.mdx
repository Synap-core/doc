# Platform Overview

Synap platform is organized as a **Product Triptych**:

- **Entities** are the typed data bricks (profiles + properties).
- **Views** are query lenses and render environments over entities.
- **Channels** connect humans, agents, and systems with governed mutations.

This architecture enables **emergent complexity**: simple building blocks compose into high-value workflows without hardcoded app silos.
