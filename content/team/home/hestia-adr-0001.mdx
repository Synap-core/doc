## Context
* The system needs a single component to enforce policy, coordinate OpenClaw ↔ Synap ↔ OpenClaude ↔ Brain calls, and produce a consistent audit trail.

## Decision options
* **Option A: Orchestrator inside Synap Backend**: tighter product coherence, fewer deployed services, easiest to keep "memory + policy" consistent.
* **Option B: Standalone `synap-orchestrator`**: clearer separation, easier to iterate routing logic independently, can be replaced without touching Synap core.

## Proposed decision (initial)
* Start with **Option B (standalone)** for faster iteration and clearer boundaries, then consider merging into Synap once interfaces stabilize.

## Consequences
* Option B adds one more service to deploy and secure, but reduces coupling and accelerates early experimentation across external channel integrations.
