## The Hub Protocol: AI Interoperability

### What Is Hub Protocol?

**Hub Protocol** is an open standard for AI services to communicate with your data pod.

**Think of it as:**

- "USB for AI" - Plug in any intelligence source
- "Docker for Agents" - Standard interface for AI services
- "OAuth for Intelligence" - Secure, scoped access

### How It Works

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ YOUR DATA POD (Open Source)                     │
│ - Your documents, tasks, contacts               │
│ - Your conversation history                     │
│ - Your knowledge graph                          │
└────────────┬────────────────────────────────────┘
             │ Hub Protocol (Standard API)
      ───────┴──────────────────────────
      │              │              │
┌─────▼─────┐  ┌────▼────┐  ┌──────▼──────┐
│ Synap AI  │  │ OpenAI  │  │ Your Custom │
│ (Default) │  │ Agents  │  │ Agents      │
└───────────┘  └─────────┘  └─────────────┘
```

**Any AI service that implements Hub Protocol can:**

- ✅ Read your data (with permission)
- ✅ Propose actions (you approve)
- ✅ Execute operations (after approval)
- ✅ Access conversation history
- ✅ Use your memory and context

**This Means:**

- ✅ **No Vendor Lock-In:** Switch AI providers anytime
- ✅ **Mix & Match:** Use multiple AI services together
- ✅ **Future-Proof:** New AIs plug in without migration
- ✅ **Competitive Market:** AIs compete for your business

### Examples

**Scenario 1: Use Synap's Hosted AI**

```
You → Data Pod → Hub Protocol → Synap Intelligence Service
(Easy, managed, 15+ agents ready)
```

**Scenario 2: Bring Your Own AI**

```

(Full control, custom logic)
```

**Scenario 3: Marketplace Agent**

```

                                ├─→ Perplexity (research)

```
