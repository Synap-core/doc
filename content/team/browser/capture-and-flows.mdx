## Primary journeys

- connect existing pod and workspace
- explore-first local onboarding before pod connection
- daily browsing and capture into entities/documents

## Capture principles

- preserve provenance (source URL, time, action)
- treat AI-assisted writes as proposal-governed
- keep local-only drafts explicit until migrated to pod
