This space answers two practical questions:

1. **Why use Synap Cloud?**  
   If you do not want to operate infrastructure, Synap Cloud handles account lifecycle, pod provisioning, connector setup flows, and subscription operations so you can focus on your data and workflows.

2. **Why use Synap Intelligence vs your own service?**  
   Synap Intelligence is the orchestration/policy layer designed for pod-native context, governed writes, and consistent agent behavior. You can still bring your own intelligence service if you want full runtime control.
