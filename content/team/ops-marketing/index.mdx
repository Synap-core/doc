# Ops & marketing

This space is the internal source of truth for operational and marketing teams working with product and engineering.

## What lives here

- Positioning and ICP definitions
- Messaging framework and narrative hierarchy
- Launch playbooks and release communications
- Sales and support enablement assets

## Working rules

1. Link every strategic claim to a supporting source (research, analytics, customer interviews, or product decision).
2. Keep one canonical page per topic. Avoid repeating the same narrative in multiple pages.
3. For every launch, define owner, audience, channels, timeline, and success metrics.
