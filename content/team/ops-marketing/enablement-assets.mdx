## Quality standard

- Every asset links back to canonical product or architecture docs. No self-contained claims that can't be traced to a source of truth.
- Every claim has one concrete proof point. "AI structures your data" → "14 system profiles, automatic classification, relation detection."
- Every asset has an owner and refresh date. Outdated assets degrade trust more than no asset at all.
- Marketing assets use language from the messaging framework — don't deviate into custom phrasing without updating the framework.
