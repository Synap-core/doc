**Eve** is Synap’s **Entity Creation System**: a **TypeScript CLI** (`eve`) and monorepo (**hestia-cli**) that turns a server into a coherent stack — **Brain** (Synap Data Pod + optional local inference), **Legs** (exposure: Traefik, tunnels), **Arms** (OpenClaw), **Eyes** (RSSHub), **Builder** (OpenCode / OpenClaude / deploy hooks). It is **not** a replacement for the Synap backend; it **orchestrates** installs and Docker sidecars alongside the official **`synap`** shell CLI.

This space is the **canonical internal narrative** for product vision, architecture, flows, and operations. **Long-form source mirrors** live in the repo ([**hestia-cli** on GitHub](https://github.com/Synap-core/hestia-cli)); this site adds structure, diagrams, and cross-links to **[Platform](/team/platform/index)**, **[Intelligence](/team/intelligence/index)**, **[DevOps](/team/devops/index)**, and **[Stack & tools](/team/technologies/index)**.

---

## North star

| Pillar | Intent |
|--------|--------|
| **Sovereignty** | Your machine, your data; optional fully local inference; no mandatory cloud for the core stack. |
| **One system** | Organs share a **mental model** (`eve` commands, `eve-network`, secrets layout) — not a random bag of installers. |
| **Synap as Brain** | The Data Pod (API, Hub, governance) is the **knowledge core**; Eve **stands it up** and wires **sidecars** and **operator tooling**. |
| **Clarity of exposure** | **Synap public URL** (Caddy) vs **Eve Legs** (Traefik / tunnel) are **separate decisions** — documented in [User flows](/team/eve-cli/user-flows). |
