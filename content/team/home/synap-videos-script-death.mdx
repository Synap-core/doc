## Asset Sourcing Guide

### 3D Models

**Where to Find:**
1. **Sketchfab** (sketchfab.com)
   - Filter: CC0 or CC-BY license
   - Search terms: "data server", "network node", "abstract cube"
   - Export as: GLTF (.glb)

2. **Poly Pizza** (poly.pizza)
   - Free low-poly models
   - Good for: Islands, cubes, geometric shapes

3. **CGTrader** (cgtrader.com)
   - Free models section
   - Search: "server", "cloud", "data center"

4. **Blender Models** (blendswap.com)
   - Free community models
   - Download as .blend, export to GLTF

**Models Needed:**
- [ ] Data pod / server cube (glowing, sci-fi)
- [ ] Network nodes (small spheres)
- [ ] Island platforms (floating)
- [ ] Abstract geometric shapes (morphing)
