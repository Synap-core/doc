# Relay Overview

Relay is the mobile-first consumer entry point for Synap.

- Built with Expo/React Native for fast cross-platform delivery.
- Targets non-developer onboarding and daily capture/interaction flows.
- Integrates with platform entities, channels, and feed surfaces.
