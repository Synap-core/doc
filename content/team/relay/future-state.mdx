# Relay future state and vision

This page is the canonical direction for where Relay is going after the initial build phase.

## Product vision

Relay is the mobile relationship and capture surface of Synap:

- Capture important moments instantly (text, voice, context).
- Turn raw input into structured, searchable knowledge.
- Keep users proactive with feed intelligence and reconnect signals.

### Historical origin

Relay concept work started from Web3 event workflows (high-intensity relationship capture), but the active product narrative is now general for knowledge workers and operators across domains.

## Target users and jobs

- Primary: builders, operators, and knowledge workers with high interaction volume.
- Core jobs:
  - capture while moving
  - recover context before follow-up
  - identify who to contact next and why

## Future state (what "good" looks like)

### Experience

- Capture is near-frictionless: text, voice, and share-based entry points.
- Person context is unified: identity, timeline, and AI context in one place.
- Feed surfaces only high-signal updates and actionable nudges.

### Platform and intelligence

- Relay uses shared Synap contracts (entities, relations, channels, proposals).
- AI enrichment is traceable and governance-aware.
- Native OS capabilities improve speed and quality without fragmenting architecture.

### Operations and shipping

- Clear release path for iOS/Android with stability and telemetry gates.
- Reproducible onboarding for connector and data sync setup.
- Tight feedback loop between product, engineering, and ops/marketing.

## Near-term priorities

1. Consolidate onboarding and first-value capture flow.
2. Improve feed relevance and person detail utility.
3. Stabilize native integrations that reduce interaction cost.
4. Align launch messaging with actual delivered capabilities.
